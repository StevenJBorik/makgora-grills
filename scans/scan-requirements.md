# Scan Requirements for Customers

Please read before submitting your dental scan.

---

## Accepted File Formats

| Format | Extension | Preferred |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| STL Binary | .stl | ✅ Best |
| STL ASCII | .stl | ✅ Good |
| PLY | .ply | ✅ Good |
| OBJ | .obj | ⚠️ OK |

**Max file size:** 50MB

---

## Quality Requirements

Your scan must include:
- ✅ Clear imprint of ALL target teeth
- ✅ Gumline visible (at least 3mm beyond teeth)
- ✅ No holes or missing surfaces
- ✅ Smooth mesh without artifacts

---

## How to Get a Scan

### Option 1: Visit a Dentist (Best Quality)
Ask for a "digital intraoral scan" in STL format.
Cost: $50-150 typically.

Compatible scanners:
- iTero Element
- 3Shape TRIOS
- Carestream CS 3600
- Primescan

### Option 2: Use Our Impression Kit
We'll mail you a kit with putty and trays.
Follow the instructions, mail it back.
We digitize it for you.

---

## File Naming

Please name your file:
```
[YourName]_[upper/lower]_[date].stl

Example: JohnSmith_upper_2026-01-11.stl
```
