## Manufacturing Notes

- **Primary material:** Black SLA resin, high-gloss finish
- **Crack inlay:** Red translucent resin OR red LED fiber (advanced)
- **Effect:** With LED fiber, can actually glow
- **Alternative:** Paint red in cracks (simpler)
