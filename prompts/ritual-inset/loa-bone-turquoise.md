- Rune voids: Flowing, organic tribal patterns

- Edge treatment: Slightly rounded (0.15mm radius)

COMFORT REQUIREMENTS:

- Ultra-light construction essential
- Retention: friction-fit at key anchor points

OUTPUT SPECIFICATION:
- Turquoise frame body: Main STL
